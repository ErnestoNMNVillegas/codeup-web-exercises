Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)



Where is Big Bend National Park located?
Texas.
How big is Big Bend?
801,163 acres.
How many people visit Big Bend?
581,000 visitors in 2021.
What is the highest elevation at Big Bend?
7,825 feet at Emory Peak.
What is the lowest elevation at Big Bend?
1,715 feet at the Rio Grande River.
What is the annual precipitation at Big Bend?
13.78 inches.
When did Big Bend become a national park?
12 June 1944.
How many archaeological sites are there at Big Bend?
It is estimated there are over 10,000 sites at Big Bend.
Any other interesting facts about Big Bend?
Big Bend has the most species of birds than any other national park.
Any other interesting facts about Big Bend?
It is larger thant the state of Rhode Island.
